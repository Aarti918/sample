The Horizon Office Tower presented unique engineering challenges due to its downtown location and ambitious design. Our team implemented innovative solutions including:

  • Advanced foundation systems for high water table
  • Custom curtain wall system for energy efficiency
  • Just-in-time material delivery in constrained urban site
  • Phased occupancy to meet tenant move-in deadlines

The project was completed on schedule and has become a benchmark for sustainable commercial development in the region.
