Our Story

Three Decades of Construction Excellence

Founded in 1992 by John and Sarah Williamson, ConstructPro began as a small residential builder with a commitment to quality craftsmanship. What started as a family business has grown into one of the region's most respected full-service construction firms.

Today, under the leadership of CEO Michael Williamson, we continue to uphold our founders' values while embracing innovation and sustainable building practices. Our portfolio now includes landmark commercial projects, luxury residences, and community developments.
